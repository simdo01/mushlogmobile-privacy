Privacy Policy for MushLogMobile

Effective Date: [Insert Date]

MushLogMobile respects your privacy. This application is designed for field data collection and does not collect, transmit, or store personal information on external servers.

Data Storage

All specimen data you record in MushLogMobile is stored locally on your device. The app does not send your information to us or to third parties.

Data Sharing

MushLogMobile allows you to export specimen data as a CSV file. This export feature is initiated by you, and you control how and where the file is shared (e.g., email, cloud storage, or transfer to another device). The app does not automatically share your data.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Your Responsibility

When exporting data, please use caution and ensure that you share it only with trusted recipients and services.
